Overview
NNRG (Nalla Narasimha Reddy Education Society's Group of Institutions) is an integrated multi-disciplinary campus established in 2009. The institute is affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE, offering Engineering, Pharmacy and Management programs.

Vision
To be a premier institution ensuring globally competent and ethically strong professionals.

Mission
- To provide higher education by refining traditional methods of teaching to make globally competent professionals.
- To impart quality education by providing state-of-the-art infrastructure and innovative research facilities.
- To practice and promote high standards of professional ethics, transparency and accountability.

Research & Incubation
- Incubation Center: Provides incubators, technical training and personality development for students.
- Robotics Development Centre: Practical application and problem-solving tasks for students.

Facilities (selected)
- Central Library: Floor area ~1000 sqm; separate lending, reading, journal and digital sections; more than 20,000 volumes covering ~2000 titles.
- Hostels: Girls hostel described; facilities include spacious rooms, study room, hot water, hygienic food and safe environment.
- Transportation: Large fleet of buses covering city routes; RTC facility mentioned.
- Laboratories: State-of-the-art labs across departments for hands-on learning.
- Sports & Games: Outdoor and indoor sports including Basketball, Cricket, Volleyball, Throw Ball, Football.
- Cafeteria: Multi-cuisine, hygienic campus cafeteria; campus store "READERS" for stationery and essentials.

Why Choose NNRG
- Integrated campus with Engineering, Pharmacy and Management schools.
- Affiliated to JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE.
- Established in 2009 with industry-aligned programs and placement focus.
