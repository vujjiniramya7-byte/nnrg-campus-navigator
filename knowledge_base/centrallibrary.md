Overview
The Central Library at NNRG provides support for teaching, learning and research. It is computerised with KOHA LMS and OPAC. Digital library systems and DELNET access are provided.

Collection (summary)
- Books: 30,000 volumes (about 5,000 titles)
- CD/DVDs: 500
- Project Reports: 400
- Print Journals: 228
- Online Journals: 600

Library Timings
- 8:30 AM to 5:00 PM on all working days

Borrowing & Services
- UG Students: 3 books for 15 days; late fee ₹1/day
- PG Students: 3 books for 15 days; late fee ₹1/day
- Teaching Staff: 4 books for one month
- Non-Teaching Staff: 2 books for one month

Digital Library
- 32 systems connected via LAN, 10 Mbps bandwidth; access to NPTEL videos and DELNET e-journals.
