Page Title: Admissions - NNRG (collected data)

Overview
- NNRG offers admissions for B.Tech, B.Pharmacy, M.Tech, MBA and M.Pharmacy programs. The institute admits students through regular entrance processes (JNTUH / state counseling) and via Management Category-B / NRI / NRI Sponsored seats.
